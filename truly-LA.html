Welcome to the home of Truly innovation

Whether dreaming up innovative new ideas or rolling out the trends that shape our culture, Los Angeles is where it all starts - and that's exactly why there's no better place for our first ever Truly Flavor Lab.

Nestled in the vibrant Downtown LA Arts District, Truly LA contains everything from our small-batch flavor innovation lab to a concert venue where we host a myriad of events ranging from weekend artist markets to live music collaborations with our friends at Live Nation. Along with serving up never-before-seen Truly concoctions, we work with local organizations, artists and musicians to create a truly one-of-a-kind experience in the beating heart of Los Angeles. Stop by for a drink (or two) and you'll see what we mean.

Guidance/Rules of the Taproom

Covid Regulations

Proof of vaccination is no longer required. Please stay home if you're experiencing any of the following symptoms: cough, shortness of breath, difficulty breathing, fever or chills.

Taproom Guidelines

First come, first serve

All seating in the Tasting Room is first come, first serve.

Counter-Service

Come on in and say hi to our friendly staff, and order all food & drinks at the bar!

Pets

Dogs are allowed outside on the tasting terrace, but must remain on a leash and be with their owners the entire time.

21+

Minors are allowed with a parent or legal guardian before 8pm during our business hours. Minors will received an “X” on their hands and are strictly prohibited from consuming alcoholic beverages on the premises.

Gender neutral restrooms - please respect everyone's right to privacy.
